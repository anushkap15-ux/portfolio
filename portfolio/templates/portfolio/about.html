Frequently asked
Simple answers to help you know me and my projects better.
What services do you offer?
I design and build responsive websites and web apps — UI/UX, frontend (HTML/CSS/JS/React) and light backend integration. For larger teams I also help with design handoffs and component libraries.
How long does a typical project take?
Small landing pages: 1–2 weeks. Medium projects: 3–6 weeks. Larger apps: 2+ months. Exact timeline depends on scope and availability — best way is to start with a brief and I'll respond with an estimated schedule.
What's your tech stack?
I primarily use modern HTML5, CSS3 (with BEM or utility classes), JavaScript (ES6+), React and occasionally Next.js for static/SSR needs. I integrate with REST APIs and Firebase for quick prototypes.
How do I get a quote?
Share a short brief — goals, references, core pages/features and your ideal timeline. Email or the contact form both work. I usually reply within 48 hours with scope options and a ballpark estimate.
Do you provide maintenance after launch?
Yes — I offer hourly or monthly maintenance packages for updates, bug fixes and small feature work. Pricing depends on response time and monthly hours required.
Can you sign an NDA?
Absolutely. I'm comfortable signing NDAs for both one-off projects and long-term partnerships.
